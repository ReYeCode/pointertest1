Guidelines

What we expect before a record gets accepted.

Recording your run

Your video needs to show the full attempt from the start of the level, with no cuts. A visible progress bar or percentage counter is required for anything under 100%.

  • Clicks or a recognizable input display should be visible somewhere on screen.
  • FPS bypass and speedhacks of any kind are an instant rejection.
  • If your run looks suspicious, we'll ask for raw, unedited footage before accepting it.

Minimum progress

Every level on the list has its own minimum percentage, shown on its page. Anything below that won't be added, even if the video is otherwise valid.

Points and scoring

Points are awarded based on where a level sits on the list. Lower rank means more points. Completing a level below 100% still earns partial points, scaled by how close you got to a full clear.

Getting added as a verifier

The first person to legitimately beat a newly placed level is credited as its verifier. Reach out through the submission form or your list's Discord to get a level considered for placement.

Appeals

If your submission gets rejected and you think that's wrong, ask the reviewer directly rather than resubmitting the same footage. Most rejections come down to something specific that's easy to fix.
